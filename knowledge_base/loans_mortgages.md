# Loans and Mortgage Terms

## Overview

First National Bank provides a comprehensive range of lending products including personal loans, auto loans, home mortgages, and home equity products. This document details our loan offerings, terms, and eligibility requirements.

## Personal Loans

### Unsecured Personal Loans

**Loan Amount**: $1,000 - $50,000
**Term Length**: 12 - 84 months
**APR Range**: 7.99% - 24.99% (based on creditworthiness)

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Minimum credit score: 640
- Minimum annual income: $20,000
- Debt-to-income ratio: Below 45%
- U.S. citizen or permanent resident
- Must be 18 years or older

**Common Uses**:
- Debt consolidation
- Major purchases
- Home improvements
- Medical expenses
- Unexpected emergencies

### Secured Personal Loans

**Loan Amount**: $5,000 - $100,000
**Term Length**: 12 - 120 months
**APR Range**: 5.99% - 18.99%

**Acceptable Collateral**:
- Savings account or CD at First National Bank
- Vehicle (model year 2015 or newer)
- Investment accounts

**Benefits**:
- Lower interest rates than unsecured loans
- Higher approval rates
- Larger loan amounts available

## Home Mortgages

### Conventional Mortgages

**Fixed-Rate Mortgage**
- Terms: 15, 20, or 30 years
- Down payment: Minimum 3% (5% preferred)
- PMI required if down payment < 20%
- Current rates (as of last update):
  - 30-year: 6.75% APR
  - 15-year: 6.00% APR

**Adjustable-Rate Mortgage (ARM)**
- 5/1 ARM: Fixed for 5 years, then adjusts annually
- 7/1 ARM: Fixed for 7 years, then adjusts annually
- Rate caps: 2% annual cap, 5% lifetime cap
- Initial rates typically 0.5-1% lower than fixed-rate

### Government-Backed Loans

**FHA Loans**
- Minimum down payment: 3.5%
- Minimum credit score: 580 (or 500 with 10% down)
- Mortgage insurance required for life of loan
- Best for: First-time homebuyers, lower credit scores

**VA Loans**
- Available to: Veterans, active-duty military, eligible spouses
- Down payment: 0% (no down payment required)
- No PMI required
- Competitive interest rates
- Funding fee: 1.4% - 3.6% (may be financed)

**USDA Loans**
- For rural and suburban properties
- Income limits apply (typically 115% of area median income)
- Down payment: 0%
- Property must be in USDA-eligible area

### Jumbo Loans

For loan amounts exceeding conforming limits:
- Current limit: $726,200 (higher in some areas)
- Down payment: Minimum 10-20%
- Credit score: Minimum 700
- Debt-to-income: Maximum 43%
- Reserves: 6-12 months of payments required

## Home Equity Products

### Home Equity Loan (HEL)

**Features**:
- Lump sum disbursement
- Fixed interest rate
- Fixed monthly payments
- Loan Amount: $10,000 - $500,000
- Term: 5 - 30 years
- Current APR: 7.25% - 12.99%

**Loan-to-Value (LTV) Limits**:
- Maximum 85% combined LTV (mortgage + HEL)
- Example: Home worth $400,000, mortgage balance $200,000
- Maximum HEL: ($400,000 Ã— 85%) - $200,000 = $140,000

### Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)

**Features**:
- Revolving credit line
- Draw period: 10 years
- Repayment period: 20 years
- Variable interest rate (Prime + margin)
- Current APR: Prime + 0.50% to Prime + 4.00%
- Credit Limit: $10,000 - $500,000

**How It Works**:
1. Apply and get approved for credit limit
2. Draw funds as needed during draw period
3. Pay interest only on amount borrowed (minimum payment)
4. After draw period, begin repaying principal + interest

## Loan Application Process

### Required Documents

**For All Loans**:
- Valid government-issued ID
- Social Security number
- Proof of income (pay stubs, W-2s, tax returns)
- Employment verification
- Bank statements (last 2-3 months)

**Additional for Mortgages**:
- Purchase agreement (if buying)
- Property information
- Homeowners insurance quote
- Gift letter (if using gifted funds)
- Explanation letters (for credit issues)

### Application Timeline

**Personal/Auto Loans**:
- Application: Same day
- Decision: 1-3 business days
- Funding: 1-5 business days after approval

**Home Mortgages**:
- Pre-approval: 1-3 days
- Full application: 1-2 weeks for initial review
- Underwriting: 2-4 weeks
- Closing: 30-45 days from application (purchase)
- Refinance: 45-60 days typical

## Interest Rate Factors

Your rate depends on:
1. **Credit Score**: Higher score = lower rate
2. **Debt-to-Income Ratio**: Lower DTI = better rates
3. **Loan-to-Value**: More equity = lower risk = better rate
4. **Loan Term**: Shorter terms often have lower rates
5. **Loan Type**: Secured loans have lower rates than unsecured
6. **Market Conditions**: Rates fluctuate with economic factors
