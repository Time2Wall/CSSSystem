### Overdraft Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | Details |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Standard Overdraft Fee | $35.00 | Per transaction when account goes negative |
| Extended Overdraft Fee | $25.00 | Charged if account negative for 5+ consecutive days |
| Maximum Daily Overdraft Fees | $105.00 | Maximum 3 overdraft fees per day |

### Overdraft Protection Options

**Overdraft Protection Transfer**
- Links checking to savings account
- Automatic transfer when checking goes negative
- Transfer fee: $10.00 per transfer
- Transfers in $100 increments

**Overdraft Line of Credit**
- Pre-approved credit line linked to checking
- Interest charged only on amount used
- APR: 18.99%
- No per-transaction fee

**Opt-Out Option**
- Decline overdraft coverage for debit card and ATM transactions
- Transactions will be declined instead of overdrafting
- Checks and ACH payments may still overdraft

## Refund Policies

### General Refund Guidelines

First National Bank may refund fees in the following situations:

1. **Bank Error**
   - Fees charged due to bank system error
   - Incorrect fee calculation
   - Full refund provided

2. **First-Time Courtesy Refund**
   - One-time courtesy refund per account type
   - Available for overdraft, NSF, or service fees
   - Request must be made within 60 days
   - Account must be in good standing

3. **Account in Good Standing**
   - Customers with 12+ months of good account history
   - No negative balances in past year
   - Eligible for periodic courtesy refunds
   - Maximum 2 refunds per calendar year

4. **Hardship Situations**
   - Job loss or medical emergency
   - Documentation may be required
   - Review on case-by-case basis

### Requesting a Refund

**Online Banking**
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Customer Service" > "Fee Refund Request"
3. Select the fee and provide reason
4. Submit request
5. Decision within 2 business days

**Phone**
1. Call 1-800-555-BANK (2265)
2. Request to speak with customer service
3. Explain situation and request refund
4. Decision provided during call or within 24 hours

**In Branch**
1. Visit any branch location
2. Speak with a personal banker
3. Bring recent statement showing the fee
4. Decision typically immediate

### Refund Processing

- Approved refunds credited within 1-2 business days
- Refunds appear as "Fee Reversal" or "Fee Refund" on statement
- Denied requests receive explanation letter/email

### Non-Refundable Fees

The following fees are generally not eligible for refund:
- Wire transfer fees (service already rendered)
- Card rush delivery fees (service already rendered)
- Safe deposit box fees (annual commitment)
- Loan late payment fees after grace period
- Account closure fees

## Fee Avoidance Tips

1. **Set up low balance alerts** - Get notified before overdraft
2. **Use direct deposit** - Often waives monthly fees
3. **Link overdraft protection** - Cheaper than overdraft fees
4. **Use our ATM network** - Over 50,000 free ATMs nationwide
5. **Go paperless** - Save $2/month on statements
6. **Monitor your accounts** - Check balances regularly via app
7. **Maintain minimum balances** - Avoid monthly maintenance fees
