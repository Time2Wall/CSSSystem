# Mobile App and Website Troubleshooting

## Overview

This guide provides solutions for common issues customers experience with First National Bank's mobile app, online banking website, and digital services. Follow these troubleshooting steps before contacting technical support.

## Mobile App Issues

### App Won't Open or Crashes

**Symptoms**: App closes immediately after opening, freezes on splash screen, or crashes during use.

**Solutions**:

1. **Force close and restart the app**
   - iPhone: Swipe up from bottom and swipe the app away
   - Android: Go to Settings > Apps > First National Bank > Force Stop

2. **Restart your device**
   - Power off completely, wait 30 seconds, power back on

3. **Check for app updates**
   - App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android)
   - Update to the latest version

4. **Clear app cache** (Android only)
   - Settings > Apps > First National Bank > Storage > Clear Cache

5. **Reinstall the app**
   - Delete the app
   - Download fresh from app store
   - Log in with your credentials

6. **Check device compatibility**
   - iOS: Requires iOS 14.0 or later
   - Android: Requires Android 8.0 or later
   - Minimum 100 MB free storage

---

### Login Problems

**"Invalid Username or Password" Error**

1. **Verify credentials**
   - Username is case-sensitive
   - Check for spaces before/after username
   - Ensure Caps Lock is off

2. **Reset password**
   - Tap "Forgot Password" on login screen
   - Enter your username and email
   - Check email for reset link (check spam folder)
   - Link expires in 24 hours

3. **Account locked**
   - After 5 failed attempts, account locks for 30 minutes
   - Wait 30 minutes and try again
   - Or call 1-800-555-TECH to unlock immediately

**Biometric Login Not Working**

1. **Re-enable biometric login**
   - Log in with password
   - Go to Settings > Security > Biometric Login
   - Toggle off, then on again
   - Re-register fingerprint/face

2. **Check device settings**
   - Ensure Face ID/Touch ID is enabled in device settings
   - Verify your biometric data is current

3. **Clean sensor**
   - Wipe fingerprint sensor or front camera with soft cloth

---

### Mobile Check Deposit Issues

**"Check Image Unclear" Error**

1. **Improve lighting**
   - Use natural light or bright indoor light
   - Avoid shadows on the check
   - Don't use flash

2. **Position check correctly**
   - Place on dark, solid background
   - Ensure all four corners are visible
   - Hold phone parallel to check (not at an angle)

3. **Clean camera lens**
   - Wipe with soft, lint-free cloth

4. **Check requirements**
   - Endorse check with signature AND "For mobile deposit only"
   - Check must be payable to you
   - Amount must match front and back

**Deposit Rejected**

Common reasons for rejection:
- Check already deposited
- Missing or incorrect endorsement
- Check is post-dated or stale-dated (over 180 days)
- Check exceeds your mobile deposit limit
- Check is damaged or altered

**Mobile Deposit Limits**:
| Account Type | Daily Limit | Monthly Limit |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| Basic Checking | $2,500 | $10,000 |
| Premium Checking | $5,000 | $25,000 |
| Business Checking | $10,000 | $50,000 |

*Limits may be increased after 6 months of account history*

---

### Push Notifications Not Receiving

1. **Check app notification settings**
   - In app: Settings > Notifications > Enable all desired alerts

2. **Check device settings**
   - iPhone: Settings > Notifications > First National Bank > Allow
   - Android: Settings > Apps > First National Bank > Notifications > Enable

3. **Disable battery optimization** (Android)
   - Settings > Battery > First National Bank > Don't optimize

4. **Check Do Not Disturb mode**
   - Disable or add First National Bank to allowed apps

## Online Banking Issues

### Cannot Access Online Banking

**"Site Cannot Be Reached" Error**

1. **Check internet connection**
   - Try loading another website
   - Restart router/modem if needed

2. **Clear browser cache**
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear Browsing Data
   - Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
   - Firefox: Options > Privacy > Clear Data

3. **Try different browser**
   - Supported browsers: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge
   - Ensure browser is updated

4. **Disable VPN**
   - Some VPNs may block banking sites

5. **Check system status**
   - Visit status.firstnationalbank.com
   - Check for scheduled maintenance

**Page Displays Incorrectly**

1. **Disable browser extensions**
   - Ad blockers can interfere with banking sites
   - Try incognito/private mode

2. **Clear cache and cookies**
   - Specifically for firstnationalbank.com

3. **Check zoom level**
   - Reset to 100% (Ctrl+0 or Cmd+0)

---

### Transfer and Payment Issues

**Transfer Not Processing**

1. **Verify account balances**
   - Ensure sufficient funds in source account
   - Check for holds on deposited funds

2. **Check transfer limits**
   - Internal transfers: $10,000/day
   - External transfers: $5,000/day
   - Wire transfers: Contact bank for limits

3. **Verify recipient information**
   - Account and routing numbers correct
   - External accounts may need re-verification

4. **Check transfer timing**
   - Internal transfers: Usually immediate
   - External transfers: 1-3 business days
   - Cut-off time: 8 PM ET for same-day processing

**Bill Pay Issues**

1. **Payment not received by payee**
   - Allow 3-5 business days for electronic payments
   - Allow 7-10 business days for check payments
   - Verify payee address is correct

2. **Wrong amount paid**
   - If payment pending: Cancel and recreate
   - If payment sent: Contact customer service for assistance

3. **Payee not found in system**
   - Add as custom payee
   - Enter payee name and address manually
   - Account number is your customer number with that payee

## Password and Security Issues

### Password Reset

**Online**:
1. Go to www.firstnationalbank.com
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter username
4. Choose verification method (email, text, or security questions)
5. Create new password

**Password Requirements**:
- 8-20 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot reuse last 6 passwords
- Cannot contain username

**Phone**:
- Call 1-800-555-TECH
- Verify identity with security questions
- Representative will send reset link

---

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Issues

**Not Receiving Security Code**

1. **Check phone number**
   - Log in > Settings > Security > Verify phone number
   - Update if incorrect

2. **Check carrier signal**
   - Ensure good cell service
   - Text messages may be delayed

3. **Request new code**
   - Wait 60 seconds
   - Click "Resend Code"

4. **Try voice call option**
   - Select "Call me instead"
   - Automated system will read code

5. **Check spam/blocked messages**
   - Codes come from short code 55123
   - Ensure not blocked

**Lost access to 2FA device**
- Call 1-800-555-TECH
- Complete enhanced verification
- Update phone number or use backup method

---

### Suspicious Activity Alert

If you receive an alert about suspicious activity:

1. **If you recognize the activity**: No action needed
2. **If you DON'T recognize it**:
   - Call immediately: 1-800-555-FRAUD
   - Do NOT click links in suspicious texts/emails
   - Lock your cards in the app

## Card Issues (Digital)

### Adding Card to Apple Pay/Google Pay

**Apple Pay**:
1. Open Wallet app
2. Tap + to add card
3. Position card in frame or enter manually
4. Verify via app, text, or call
5. Card ready to use

**Google Pay**:
1. Open Google Pay app
2. Tap + Add to Wallet > Payment card
3. Scan card or enter manually
4. Verify via app, text, or call
5. Card ready to use

**Verification Failed**:
- Call 1-800-555-CARD to verify manually
- Ensure card is not expired
- Card must be activated first

### Card Controls (Lock/Unlock)

**In Mobile App**:
1. Go to Cards section
2. Select your card
3. Toggle "Lock Card" on/off

**What happens when locked**:
- All new transactions declined
- Recurring payments may be declined
- Existing authorizations may still post
