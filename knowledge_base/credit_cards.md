## Credit Limits

### How Credit Limits Are Determined

Credit limits are based on:
1. **Credit Score**: Higher scores qualify for higher limits
2. **Income**: Higher income supports larger credit lines
3. **Debt-to-Income Ratio**: Lower DTI allows for more credit
4. **Credit History**: Length and quality of credit history
5. **Existing Relationship**: Current customers may receive higher limits

### Credit Limit Increases

**Automatic Increases**:
- Account in good standing for 6+ months
- Consistent on-time payments
- Regular card usage
- Reviewed automatically every 6 months

**Request an Increase**:
1. Log in to online banking
2. Go to Credit Cards > Request Limit Increase
3. Provide updated income information
4. Decision within 2-3 business days
5. Hard inquiry may be performed

**Typical Increase Amounts**: 10-25% of current limit

## Fraud Protection

### Zero Liability Policy

You are not responsible for unauthorized charges made with your card, whether:
- Card is lost or stolen
- Card number is used fraudulently online
- Card is skimmed or cloned

**Your Responsibility**: Report lost/stolen cards immediately

### Fraud Monitoring

Our 24/7 fraud monitoring system:
- Analyzes every transaction in real-time
- Flags unusual spending patterns
- Sends instant alerts for suspicious activity
- May temporarily freeze card if fraud suspected

### Fraud Alerts

Set up customizable alerts for:
- Any transaction over your specified amount
- Card-not-present (online) transactions
- International transactions
- ATM withdrawals

### What to Do If You Suspect Fraud

1. **Immediately call**: 1-800-555-FRAUD (3728) - Available 24/7
2. **Lock your card**: Use the mobile app to instantly freeze your card
3. **Review transactions**: Identify all unauthorized charges
4. **File dispute**: We'll investigate and issue provisional credit
5. **Get new card**: Replacement card shipped within 1-2 business days

### Investigation Process

- Provisional credit issued within 10 business days for most disputes
- Investigation completed within 45 days (90 days for new accounts)
- You'll be notified of outcome in writing
- If fraud confirmed, credit becomes permanent

## Dispute Process

### Types of Disputes

**Fraud Disputes**
- Unauthorized transactions
- Identity theft
- Skimming/card cloning

**Billing Disputes**
- Charged wrong amount
- Charged twice for same purchase
- Never received goods/services
- Goods significantly different than described
- Returned item but not credited

### How to File a Dispute

**Online**:
1. Log in to online banking
2. Go to Credit Cards > Transaction History
3. Click on transaction > "Dispute This Charge"
4. Provide details and documentation
5. Submit dispute

**Phone**: Call 1-800-555-CARD (2273)

**Mail**: Download dispute form and mail with documentation to:
First National Bank
Card Disputes Department
P.O. Box 12345
Anytown, USA 12345

### Dispute Timeline

- File within 60 days of statement date
- Investigation takes 30-45 days
- Provisional credit while investigating
- Final decision in writing
