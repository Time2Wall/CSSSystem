# Branch Locations and Contact Information

## Overview

First National Bank operates over 500 branches across the Eastern United States, with extended hours at select locations and full-service capabilities at all branches. This document provides information about our branch network, services, and contact channels.

## Branch Services

### Available at All Branches

- Account opening (personal and business)
- Deposits and withdrawals
- Check cashing
- Bill payments
- Wire transfers (domestic and international)
- Cashier's checks and money orders
- Notary services
- Safe deposit boxes
- Loan applications
- Credit card applications
- Account maintenance and inquiries
- Debit card services (replacements, PIN changes)

### Available at Select Branches

- Investment services (financial advisors)
- Mortgage specialists
- Business banking specialists
- Foreign currency exchange
- Drive-through banking
- Saturday hours
- Extended weekday hours
- Coin counting machines
