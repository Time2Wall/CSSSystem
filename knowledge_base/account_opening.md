# Account Opening Policies

## Overview

First National Bank offers a variety of account types to meet your financial needs. This document outlines the requirements and procedures for opening new accounts.

## Account Types

### Personal Checking Account

**Basic Checking**
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- Monthly maintenance fee: $5 (waived with $500 minimum daily balance)
- Free debit card included
- Online and mobile banking access
- Unlimited transactions

**Premium Checking**
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with $2,500 minimum daily balance)
- Free checks
- No foreign ATM fees (up to $20/month reimbursed)
- Priority customer service line
- Free cashier's checks

**Student Checking**
- Available for students aged 17-24 with valid student ID
- No minimum balance requirement
- No monthly maintenance fee
- Free debit card
- Converts to Basic Checking after age 25 or graduation

### Savings Accounts

**Regular Savings**
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Monthly maintenance fee: $3 (waived with $300 minimum balance)
- Current APY: 0.50%
- 6 free withdrawals per month; $5 fee for additional withdrawals

**High-Yield Savings**
- Minimum opening deposit: $1,000
- No monthly maintenance fee with $5,000 minimum balance
- Current APY: 2.75%
- 3 free withdrawals per month; $10 fee for additional withdrawals

**Money Market Account**
- Minimum opening deposit: $2,500
- Current APY: 3.25% (tiered based on balance)
- Check-writing privileges (3 checks per month)
- Debit card available

## Required Documents

### For Individual Accounts

To open any personal account, you must provide:

1. **Valid Government-Issued Photo ID** (one of the following):
   - State driver's license
   - State ID card
   - U.S. passport
   - U.S. military ID

2. **Social Security Number** - required for tax reporting purposes

3. **Proof of Address** (one of the following, dated within 60 days):
   - Utility bill (electric, gas, water, phone)
   - Bank or credit card statement
   - Lease agreement
   - Mortgage statement
   - Government-issued document (tax form, benefits letter)

4. **Initial Deposit** - cash, check, or transfer from existing account

### For Joint Accounts

Both account holders must:
- Be present at account opening (or complete remote verification)
- Provide all documents listed above
- Decide on account ownership type (Joint Tenants with Rights of Survivorship or Tenants in Common)

### For Business Accounts

Additional requirements:
- Articles of Incorporation or Organization
- Business license
- Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- Resolution authorizing account opening
- Operating Agreement (for LLCs)

## Account Opening Process

### In-Branch Opening

1. Visit any First National Bank branch during business hours
2. Inform the greeter you wish to open a new account
3. Meet with a personal banker
4. Review account options and select the best fit
5. Complete application form
6. Provide required documents for verification
7. Make initial deposit
8. Receive temporary debit card (permanent card mailed within 7-10 days)
9. Set up online banking credentials

**Typical time required**: 30-45 minutes

### Online Account Opening

1. Visit www.firstnationalbank.com/open-account
2. Select account type
3. Complete online application
4. Upload photos of required documents
5. Verify identity through our secure process
6. Fund account via transfer or debit card
7. Account typically approved within 1-2 business days

**Note**: Some account types and situations may require in-branch verification.

## Age Requirements

- **Individual accounts**: Must be 18 years or older
- **Student accounts**: 17-24 years with valid student ID
- **Minor accounts (Custodial)**: Parent or guardian opens account on behalf of minor; child gains full control at age 18 (or 21 in some states)

## Account Restrictions

### Customers Who May Not Open Accounts

We cannot open accounts for:
- Non-U.S. residents without valid visa and U.S. address
- Individuals on the OFAC sanctions list
- Individuals with previous account fraud at our bank
- Individuals in ChexSystems with unresolved negative history

### ChexSystems Review

All applications are subject to ChexSystems review. If you have a negative ChexSystems report:
- You may be offered a Second Chance Checking account
- Second Chance accounts have limited features and higher fees
- After 12 months of good standing, you may upgrade to regular checking

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I open an account if I'm visiting from another state?**
A: Yes, as long as you have a valid U.S. address where we can send correspondence.

**Q: How long does it take to get my debit card?**
A: A temporary card is provided at account opening. Your permanent card arrives by mail in 7-10 business days.

**Q: Can I open an account for someone else?**
A: No, except for custodial accounts for minors or legal guardianship situations with proper documentation.

**Q: What if I don't have a Social Security Number?**
A: We can accept an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) for non-citizens.
